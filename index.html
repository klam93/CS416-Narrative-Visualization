Military Expenditure vs. GDP by Country

This visualization explores the relationship between a country's GDP and its military expenditure as a percentage of GDP. The data spans from 1960 to 2022, allowing for a historical comparison of military spending trends across different regions and countries.

The circles represent individual countries, colored by their respective regions. Hover over a circle to see detailed information about the country, including its GDP and military expenditure.

Please use the "Next," "Previous," and "Start Over" buttons to navigate through the scenes. At the end of the overview, you can use the slider to select a specific year or click on a region to explore the data for that particular grouping.
